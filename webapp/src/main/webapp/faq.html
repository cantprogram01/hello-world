Frequently Asked Questions

Don't worry, your Workers will continue to work once your productivity is full. The 24hr timer is simply for your convenience.

Over Time! Miners are not bought, Workers are hired therefore they can't be sold. Once Workers are hired they work for you indefinitely, providing you with an unlimited amount of BNB at a starting rate of 3% daily.



How much are my fees?

The only fee that users personally incur is their transaction gas fees, which is charged by the BSC network.



When is the best time to Hire Workers?

Always! No matter when you hire Workers, you will begin with a return rate of 3% daily.



How often should I compound?

WE recommend that you compound at least once per day but you are free to do so as often as you wish.



How is BNBWorker sustainable?

BNB Worker is sustained by continued community support, just as every other crypto coin, token or project. The difference is BNB Worker also has an algorithm that doesn't allow others to instantly dump their coins on the community.



How to use BNBWorker on my phone?

You can either use an app with a dapp browser and choose the Metamask/Injected option or simply use any browser that you want.



Is it better to compound or pocket?

By design, compounding will be more profitable than pocketing in the long-term.



How do referrals work?

When a new user hires Workers after clicking your personal referral link, the contract will send a BNB value equal to 10% instantly to your barrel. Be smart, use it to hire Workers and build yourself a stream of income.
